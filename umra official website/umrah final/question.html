Frequently Asked Questions

What are the rules for Umrah?

Umrah requires Muslims to perform two key rituals, Tawaf and Sa'i . Tawaf is a circling round the Ka'bah (Arabic : كَـعْـبَـة‎, "House of God"). For men, it is recommended to do the first three circuits in a hurried pace, followed by four rounds of a more leisurely pace.

The best time to perform Umrah is the month of December because weather is cold and hotels rate are comparatively low in this month as compare to other months. So, people who cannot bear hot weather can go in December to perform Umrah. Most of the people usually perform Umrah in this month .

What is the dress code for Umrah?

It is not mandatory for women to wear white dress during Umrah as there is no specific dress code for Umrah or Hajj for women. Women pilgrims can use any of their regular clothes but mostly women prefer to wear white clothes during their holy trip.

Is Ihram compulsory for Umrah?

In order to perform Umrah or Hajj, a Muslim man or woman has to enter Ihram. Without Ihram, one's Umrah or Hajj cannot be performed. ... However, they also have to wear special clothing that is designed for the purpose of performing Umrah or Hajj.

How much money can you carry to Saudi Arabia?

As per Saudi Customs rules and regulations, all passengers traveling from/to Saudi Arabia carrying cash or jewelry or Bearer-Negotiable Instruments (BNI's) worth 60,000 SAR or more, must fill a declaration form and hand it to the Customs officer at the airports.

Very unlike men, women can wear stitched clothing. Silk cannot be worn during Umrah. Jewelry is not permissible during performance of Umrah. Women do not need to cover her face for Umrah.

What is not allowed in Ihram?

During the pilgrimage, sexual activity, smoking and swearing are also forbidden. Other forbidden activities include killing animals, using profane language, quarrelling or fighting, and taking oaths, in addition to any other regularly prohibited acts. Males should also refrain from looking at women.

Can we do 2 Umrah a day?

Yes, it is perfectly permissible for anyone who comes to Makkah for the Umrah to perform more than one Umrah on the same journey. However, he must start his second Umrah from a point outside the Haram area. ... Unless we are doing the second Umrah on behalf of someone else, then we should not.

When can I perform Umrah?

We believe that pilgrims have a right to know that Islam does not specify any particular dates to perform Umrah; you can perform it anytime of the year. However, Saudi authorities do not issue visas to foreign travellers three months prior to Hajj i.e. Shawwal, Dhul Qadah and Dhul Hajj.

It is always possible to perform Umrah in one day. No doubt about it. But you must prepare for it. Preparing is to having prepared the documents and having adequate knowledge to perform an Umrah.
